How To Play:

At the start of the game you must make a bet with the money you have

After makeing your bet, you will be given a hand of 5 cards. You can exchange each card once for a new card

When you have a hand youre satisfied with or you have exchanged each card select the keep cards button and see if you have a winning hand.

Payouts for each hand:

|Pair - 1:1| |Two Pair - 5:1| |Three of a Kind - 10:1| |Straight - 25:1| |Flush - 50:1| |Full House - 100:1| |Four of a Kind - 1,000:1| |Straight Flush - 10,000:1| |Royal Flush - 100,000:1|
